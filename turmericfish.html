Vietnamese Turmeric Fish

Servings: 4 / Estimated Time: 40 - 45 mins

Ingredients:

Turmeric Fish:

  • 1 lb tilapia, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp shallots, minced
  • 1 tbsp dill, minced
  • 1 stalk green onion, minced

Toppings:

  • 1 package vermicelli noodles
  • 1 head lettuce
  • mint
  • 2 tbsp peanuts, toasted and chopped
  • 1/3 cup Vietnamese dipping sauce
  • 2 tbsp fried shallots, optional

Directions:

  1. Add the tilapia to a bowl with all the ingredients through the fish sauce. Mix and marinate for 15 minutes. Make sure the fish is coated in the marinade.

  2. While the tilapia is marinating, prep your other ingredients. Mince your shallots, dill, and green onions. Cook your vermicelli noodles.

  3. After the tilapia is done marinating, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium high heat. Add in the shallots and cook for a minute until they are shiny. Add the tilapia and marinade to the pan. Cook the tilapia for 2 minutes on each side.

  4. Add in the dill and green onion. Cook for another 30 seconds. Remove from the heat.

  5. Assemble your vermicelli bowl! Add vermicelli and lettuce to a bowl. Top with tilapia, fried shallots, mint and peanuts. Drizzle Vietnamese dipping sauce over the top and serve.
